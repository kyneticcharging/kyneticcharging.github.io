Kynetic is a platform that allows households to set up charging stations for electric car owners. Our mission is to solve the issue of industry-crippling inefficiencies of charging stations so that the great advances of battery technology won't to waste.

Our company will ship any household that applies an electric vehicle cable and charger which they will pay for. They will make a set revenue per minute of charging. An electric car owner will see the household displayed on the map. Since the app will be synced with the car, we will maximize the distance the car can make to a charging station. Once the driver manually selects the location, the top of the charger will pop off and the charger will start to draw electricity from the house. Since this will obviously be costly to any household, our algorithm will ensure a minimum level of profit for each household and adjust price accordingly.
